Low Carb Chocolate Frosting

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • Sprinkle of Cocoa Powder
  • 2 packets of Splenda
Whip the heavy whipping cream until it is fluffy. Then add the other ingredients. Be careful not to whip too much.

Low Carb Jello Frosting

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • Sprinkle of you favorite sugar-free Jello Gelatin Powder
Whip the heavy whipping cream until it is fluffy. Then add the other ingredients. Be careful not to whip too much.
